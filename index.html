Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right course for me?

Discover your passions, evaluate your strengths, and match them with potential career paths. The right course aligns your interests with future opportunities.

Do I get a Certificate for every course I take?

Certificate availability depends on the course provider. Some courses include certificates, while others may not. Check course details.

Do I get refund if the course isn't worth my money?

Refund policies vary by provider. Check the course terms or contact customer support to confirm if refunds are available.

Will I have lifetime access to course I purchase?

Lifetime access to a course depends on the provider’s policy. Verify with the course details or terms for confirmation.

What are your payment options?

Payment options typically include credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Check the specific service or platform for available methods.

Do I get all source code from projects?

Access to source code varies: open-source projects offer it freely, while proprietary projects often restrict access. Check project terms.
